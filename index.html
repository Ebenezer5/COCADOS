Our Story

COCADOS was founded on September 26, 2017, with a vision to transform the way people access healthy, nutritious, and convenient food. The company was born out of a realization by its CEO and founder that, in many communities, finding wholesome and healthy food options was a significant challenge.

We strive to make healthy eating enjoyable and accessible to all, ensuring our products are always fresh, nutrient-dense, and full of flavor. Whether you're ordering a cake for a special occasion or grabbing a healthy snack, COCADOS is your go-to destination for delicious and nutritious food.
