Our Story

Transforming food culture since 2017

Who We Are

COCADOS is revolutionizing access to healthy, nutritious, and convenient food options in Ghana and beyond.

Our Journey

2017 - The Beginning

Founded on September 26, 2017 with a vision to transform food accessibility. Recognized the challenge of finding wholesome food options in many communities.

2018 - First Expansion

Hired first two staff members to handle deliveries while the founder focused on cooking. Established our reputation for quality and reliability.

2019 - Significant Growth

Opened new branch at Market Circle, Takoradi. Officially launched under the COCADOS brand name. Expanded team with three additional staff members.

Present Day

Continuing to innovate and expand our offerings while maintaining our commitment to health, convenience, and quality.

The Challenge We Faced

Unhealthy Norms

Communities dominated by unhealthy options like fried plantains with excess oil, overly spiced stews, and processed foods with adverse health effects.

Operational Delays

Businesses facing disruptions due to long-distance food sourcing and inconsistent quality.

Our Solution

Fresh Ingredients

Using fresh, high-quality ingredients while minimizing excess oil, salt, and unhealthy additives.

Balanced Meals

Creating dishes that are both healthy and flavorful, catering to specific dietary needs.

Reliable Delivery

Bridging the gap between quality food preparation and timely delivery.

Our Mission

To inspire a lifestyle of healing and sound mind in men and women through nutritious food choices.

Our Vision

To establish COCADOS as the prime destination for wholesome, nourishing yet tasty meals beyond Ghana.
